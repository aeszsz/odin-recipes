Hot Chocolate

Description

Ahh... I'm feeling cozy just thinking about this drink. Hot chocolate is something you'd see me drinking all throughout the year, not just in the winter! It's sweet and warm, and can be a great company to my apple pie!

You can make it as milky as you like. You can add cinnamon, marshmallows, whipped cream or some fruit. I'm sure you'll love it!

Ingredients

  • 1 bar of chocolate, chopped
  • 2/3 cup milk, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place chocolate in a saucepan over medium-low heat.
  3. Slowly add milk and whisk constantly until chocolate in melted and well blended.
  4. Whisk in cinnamon.
  5. Remove from heat and add more milk if desired.
  6. Serve in a mug.
